Perry Plat E. Pus

Perry Plat E. Pus founded MakeLabs in 1987, and singlehandedly pioneered the gaming industry as we know it. He gave Nintendo the idea for Mario, Sony the idea for Kratos, and created a cult following where people willingly love to work for his corperation. With only four games out, what he has accomplished is surely a feat. Growing up in New Jersey, he made a massive name and business for himself. Struggling to find food, he had to resort to newspapers. He sold so many newspapers, the newspapers became about him! After that, he was famous. He was the world's first celebrity. He was rich, cool, famous, and everybody liked him.

When he passed in 2021 after catching a virus, the world truly suffered a massive loss. His funeral was attended by thousands. Nobody stayed home, everybody came to pay their respects. We will not forget you, Perry Plat E. Pus. You were a great man.
